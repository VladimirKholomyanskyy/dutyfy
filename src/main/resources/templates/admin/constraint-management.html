What are Admin Constraints?

Admin constraints are hard constraints that cannot be violated when creating schedules. These include:

  • Religious holidays - Days when specific employees cannot work due to religious observances
  • Medical appointments - Scheduled medical procedures or treatments
  • Mandatory training - Required training sessions or conferences
  • Legal restrictions - Court dates, jury duty, etc.
Note: Unlike employee preferred off dates (which are soft constraints), admin constraints are absolute and will cause schedule creation to fail if they cannot be satisfied.
Current Sample Constraints (from DataLoader):
  • John Doe - Good Friday (April 18, 2025)
  • Jane Smith - Rosh Hashanah (September 15, 2025)
  • Mike Johnson - Christmas (December 25, 2025)
